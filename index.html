ABOUT ME

My name is Jana (pronounced like "Jenna") and I love all things creative! I have a Bachelor's in Information Science and a Master's in Human-Computer Interaction - fancy ways of saying Data Science and UX Design/Research, respectively. UX design is a passion of mine because it not only serves as a creative outlet for me, but also ensures accessibility and usability in day-to-day interfaces. Before any aesthetic decisions that I make in my designs, I always consider user needs first and foremost; because at the end of the day, no users means no interface.
As a data scientist/UX designer mix, I understand both the back and front-end of the projects that I am faced with. I have been a core member of each part of the design and implementation process - from researching to prototyping to testing to implementing. I work heavily with multiple tools to provide users with the information that they need; including Figma, Python, ReactJS, HTML/CSS, and PowerBI.
When I'm not designing, I love playing video games, painting, watching anime, taking a nap with my 2 cats, or traveling! I've been to 27 states, 15 countries (4 of which I've scuba dived in!), and 8 national parks. I also love trying new hobbies (and abandoning them halfway through...) like pottery, crocheting, or skateboarding.
Thank you for taking the time to learn more about me! Head on over to "My Work" to see some of my projects if you haven't already :)
